Multan

Multan is located in Punjab, and covers an area of 560 square kilometres (220 sq mi). The nearest important cities
are Dera Ghazi Khan and Bahawalpur. Multan is located in a bend created by five rivers of central Pakistan. The
Sutlej River separates it from Bahawalpur and the Chenab River from Muzaffar Garh. The area around the city is a
flat, alluvial plain surrounded by orchards and deserts that is used for citrus and mango farms.

Multan is surrounded by Sulaiman mountain range which is the extension of Southern Hindu Kush mountain range
of Pakistan and Afghanistan. The most well-known peak of the Sulaiman mountains is the twin-peaked Takht-e-
